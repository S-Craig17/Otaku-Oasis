Featured Manga

Tongari Boushi no Atelier

Coco, a dressmaker’s daughter, is fascinated by magic and witches. When she spies on the witch Qifrey, she discovers her favorite picture book is actually a magic book and starts experimenting with spells.

After a spell causes a disaster at home, Qifrey rescues Coco and decides to train her. He believes she can help him track down the Brimmed Caps, a dangerous group of heretics. But first, Coco must improve her magical skills and get along with Qifrey’s other apprentices.
